# Healthcare IT News Feature: June 2025

**Headline:** *How Dr. Jessica Edwards Deployed AI Reception to Cut Overhead by 30%*

**Summary:**
Solo practices and small clinic networks are struggling with administrative fatigue, leading to high burnout rates and closure rates of 5–8% annually. Dr. Jessica Edwards, DO MBA, board-certified family medicine physician and founder of Zara Medical, designed a custom AI-native system to streamline her clinic's patient reception, scheduling, and charting pipelines.

**Key Metrics Achieved:**
- **Inbound Overhead Reductions:** Deployed AI Receptionist successfully automated 70% of inbound calls.
- **Provider Time Savings:** Restored over 2 hours of direct clinical time per provider every business day.
- **Patient Satisfaction:** Patient survey scores increased by 40% due to conversational voice pipelines.
